Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find a ride?

Go to the Find Ride page, enter your pickup and destination, and view available rides.

How do I offer a ride?

Click on Offer Ride, enter route details and vehicle info, then publish your ride.

How do I verify my account?

Go to your Profile page and complete mobile and ID verification steps.

What if a ride gets cancelled?

You will be notified instantly. You can search for another ride anytime.
